About Institute,
Dr. D. Y. Patil School of Design

Established in 2022, DPU | Dr. D. Y. Patil School of Design enriches the legacy of Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune, with a focus on exceptional design education. Our programs empower students to creatively solve real-world design challenges by blending innovation, technical skill, and design empathy. We offer an AICTE-approved Bachelor of Design with specializations in Fashion Design, Communication Design, Interior Spatial & Furniture, Product Design, UX Design and Animation & VFX Design. Supported by cutting-edge facilities and guided by experts from premier institutes like NID and IIT, our faculty fosters a dynamic learning environment shaping the institute as best design institutes across India. DPU SOD strives to be a leading hub for design innovation and education, nurturing the next generation of design leaders in India.

About University,
Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth

The beginning in 2000, Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pimpri, Pune (DPU) was established under the leadership of the great visionary and philanthropist, Dr. D. Y. Patil Sir.
Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pimpri, Pune is a private, non-profit, self-financing organization, dedicated to serving society and making a difference. Initially, it had a humble beginning with only one institute under its fold i.e. Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical, College & Research Centre. The Institute started as a Centre of Excellence with emphasis on medical education, research and healthcare. Within a short span of time, the institute gained recognition as one of the best in the State and across the country. Gradually, over the last 20 years, there has seen a humongous growth and 14 more professional institutes have been added under its umbrella.

Programs Offered

B.Des in Communication Design

Exam Accepted : DPU AIDAT | UCEED | NID

Eligibility: Candidates must be 10 + 2 from any discipline and from a recognized university or board with a minimum of 45% for Open and minimum of 40% for others. Freshers may also apply.

Duration: 4 years full-time

Program Highlights

The B.Des in Communication Design at DPU School of Design shapes students into visual storytellers, strategic thinkers, and creators capable of influencing perception through design. With its contemporary and industry-aligned curriculum, the program stands among the notable b.des communication design options in India.
Students explore how visuals, messages, and media work together across print and digital platforms. The program emphasizes user-centric, culturally aware, and strategically driven communication.
Key domains include Branding & Identity Design, Graphic Design, Typography, Illustration, Motion Graphics, Digital Media, Advertising, Visual Strategy, and UI/UX. With strong exposure to digital ecosystems apps, websites, interfaces students develop future-ready skills aligned with industry demands.
The holistic curriculum builds creativity, technical skill, and strategic clarity. Graduates pursue roles such as Graphic Designer, Visual Communication Designer, Brand Identity Designer, UI/UX Designer, Motion Graphic Artist, Illustrator, Art Director, Digital Content Designer, and more. DPU's multidisciplinary campus places it among the leading communication design course destinations for aspiring designers.

M.Des in Communication Design

Exam Accepted : CEED

Eligibility: Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in Design, Fine Arts, Architecture, Engineering, or related fields with a minimum of 45% marks for Open category and 40% marks for other categories. Freshers may also apply.

Duration: 2 years full-time

Program Highlights

The M.Des in Communication Design empowers students to create meaningful and impactful visual experiences. Rooted in creativity and strategy, the program teaches learners to translate complex ideas into compelling visual narratives across diverse media.
Students learn to see design as a problem-solving discipline, understanding audience perception, storytelling ethics, and communication dynamics. Through real-world projects, research-based assignments, and collaborative workshops, they develop both conceptual clarity and practical mastery.
Key focus areas include typography, color theory, layout, imagery, motion graphics, digital media, branding, advertising, and UX-driven communication. This blend positions the program among the strong choices for aspiring professionals pursuing masters in communication design.
Graduates emerge ready to work in advertising, digital media, publishing, corporate branding, UI/UX design, and communication-driven roles where visual impact matters.

About Global Business School and Research Centre

Global Business School & Research Centre (GBSRC) situated at Tathawade, Pune is the flagship institute of Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune. It was established in 2006. In a span of 19 years, the Institute has carved a name for itself amongst the top business schools of the country.
The idea of setting up this Institute is to offer ‘Management Education’ to aspiring leaders of tomorrow. From this institute, students can pursue their graduate, under graduate and Ph. D. programs in management. GBSRC offers 2 years full time MBA program which is approved by AICTE, Government of India, New Delhi. It also offers 3 years full time BBA program and Ph. D. programme in management.
The main mission of DPU is to groom students who can turn into intelligent leaders. After they pass out of the Institute, they in turn will be able to source information from diverse resources and administer it for the benefit of business and society at large.

B.Com with Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)

CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) program is a unique blend of academic excellence and globally recognized professional certification. This integrated pathway empowers students to earn their undergraduate degree while simultaneously preparing for the CFA Program, the world’s most respected credential in investment management and financial analysis. The Integrated B. Masters of Business Administration

Welcome to the MBA Program at GBSRC, a prestigious 2-year full-time course approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Government of India. The program is structured over four semesters, laying a strong foundation with common core courses in the first semester. From the second semester onwards, students choose specializations designed to cultivate domain-specific expertise and prepare focused professionals for the dynamic business landscape.

At GBSRC, we are committed to academic excellence, research innovation, and staying ahead of business trends. Our pioneering AI-Embedded MBA program is a forward-thinking initiative that equips students not just to enter the industry, but to lead and redefine it. In an era driven by artificial intelligence, analytics, and digital disruption, our curriculum seamlessly integrates AI, data science, and emerging technologies, aligning with the demands of the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

Our vision is to become a globally recognized management institution, shaping responsible and future-ready leaders who are technologically adept and ethically grounded. Through a holistic, interdisciplinary pedagogy, we blend AI literacy with managerial skills to empower students to tackle real-world challenges and excel in an AI-enabled business environment. The GBSRC MBA attracts ambitious students nationwide due to our reputation for nurturing agile professionals with the ideal combination of knowledge, skills, and attitude. Our esteemed faculty team, comprising distinguished academicians, researchers, and industry experts, delivers a rich learning experience enhanced by AI-driven teaching methods, digital simulations, and project-based learning.

Our teaching approach emphasizes global best practices with case-based learning to foster participation, critical thinking, and innovation. By incorporating AI tools in decision-making, predictive analytics, and digital strategy, our MBA graduates are fully prepared to drive growth and transformation across diverse industries.

Join GBSRC’s AI-Embedded MBA to become a visionary leader of tomorrow’s business world.

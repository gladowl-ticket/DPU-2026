About Institute,
Dr. D. Y. Patil School of Design

Established in 2022, DPU | Dr. D. Y. Patil School of Design enriches the legacy of Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune, with a focus on exceptional design education. Our programs empower students to creatively solve real-world design challenges by blending innovation, technical skill, and design empathy. We offer an AICTE-approved Bachelor of Design with specializations in Fashion Design, Communication Design, Interior Spatial & Furniture, Product Design, UX Design and Animation & VFX Design. Supported by cutting-edge facilities and guided by experts from premier institutes like NID and IIT, our faculty fosters a dynamic learning environment shaping the institute as best design institutes across India. DPU SOD strives to be a leading hub for design innovation and education, nurturing the next generation of design leaders in India.

About University,
Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth

The beginning in 2000, Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pimpri, Pune (DPU) was established under the leadership of the great visionary and philanthropist, Dr. D. Y. Patil Sir.
Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pimpri, Pune is a private, non-profit, self-financing organization, dedicated to serving society and making a difference. Initially, it had a humble beginning with only one institute under its fold i.e. Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical, College & Research Centre. The Institute started as a Centre of Excellence with emphasis on medical education, research and healthcare. Within a short span of time, the institute gained recognition as one of the best in the State and across the country. Gradually, over the last 20 years, there has seen a humongous growth and 14 more professional institutes have been added under its umbrella.

Programs Offered

B.Des in Interior Spatial and Furniture Design

Exam Accepted : DPU AIDAT | UCEED | NID


Eligibility : Candidate with minimum of 50% in 10 + 2 from any stream and recognized
board is eligible to apply. Candidates appearing for 10 + 2 may also apply


Duration : 4-year full-time

Program Highlights

The Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Interior, Spatial and Furniture Design is a 4-year full-time programme that builds a strong foundation in spatial planning, furniture design, materials, and human-centered design. The curriculum integrates studio-based learning, sustainable practices, and digital tools such as BIM and VR. The programme culminates in a capstone project and public exhibition, preparing graduates for careers in design practice, entrepreneurship, or advanced postgraduate studies

B.Sc. Hons. in Interior Design


Eligibility : Candidates must be 10 + 2 from any discipline and from a recognized university or board with a minimum of 45% for Open and minimum of 40% for others. Freshers may also apply.


Duration : 4 years full-time

Program Highlights

The B.Sc. (Hons.) in Interior Design integrates conceptual thinking, floor plans, site assessment, materials science, and spatial implementation through a student-centered learning approach. The program encourages learners to develop innovative solutions in a rapidly evolving design ecosystem where challenges and opportunities coexist.
Students learn to combine real-world needs with creative solutions to shape meaningful spatial experiences. Mentorship from industry professionals and hands-on involvement in live projects strengthen both practical and conceptual understanding.

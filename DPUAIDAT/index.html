DPU All India Design Aptitude Test (DPU AIDAT)


DPU All India Design Aptitude Test is designed in a multiple-choice question format with a set time limit to understand the design acumen and accuracy and the time management of the prospect candidature.

The test is designed and developed by Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pimpri, Pune for its constituent unit DPU School of Design, Tathawade campus. It is important that applicants take the test which helps us further to onboard the best potential for the design programs. DPU All India Design Aptitude Test is an Online Proctored Test that allows candidates to appear at their convenient location. Candidates are not expected to go to a centre and neither it is paper based.

Important Instructions For DPU AIDAT

  • DPU All India Design Aptitude Test is Online Proctored based and conducted by Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth for its School of Design, Pune | India.
  • Admit cards can be downloaded from dpu.edu.in one week prior to the DPU All India Design Aptitude Test (DPU AIDAT). Kindly take coloured and clear print of the admit card for the verification during online DPU AIDAT.
  • DPU AIDAT is scheduled to start at 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM on 19th April, 2026.
  • Total duration of the DPU All India Design Aptitude Test is 90 minutes.
  • Candidates must be seated at least 15 minutes prior to the DPU AIDAT is scheduled.
  • Candidates must ensure that the system is on, duly charged, high bandwidth internet connection and the camera is in working condition throughout the Test.
  • Candidates must ensure all the necessary material is readily in possession. Candidates are not allowed to speak or call for assistance during the Test.
  • Candidates must keep their admit card, Aadhar Card / Driving Licence as ID proof, if asked for verification.
  • SECTION - A B C will have Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) and candidates have to select an appropriate (correct) answer for each question. Each question has only one correct answer.
  • Each question is of one mark and there are no negative marks for a wrong answer.
  • Candidates should be seated in an isolated room without any disturbance during the test. Once candidates are given access, they will not be allowed to leave their seat till the test is over and submitted.
  • For any suspicious action, movement or prompts detected by the proctor, the candidate will be disqualified from the test immediately without any warning.
  • Use of calculators, digital watches during the test are strictly not allowed. If found detected, candidate will be disqualified without any further warning.
  • Use of Geometry box or Scale are strictly not allowed during the test assignment.
  • For any query related to technical errors, downloading admit cards, forgot username or password, etc; kindly connect with our counsellor immediately.

Important FAQs

When will the application form open for the DPU AIDAT 2026 exam?
The application form for DPU AIDAT 2026 exam is currently open. The application can be submitted in online mode only. The last date to fill out the DPU AIDAT application form is APRIL 10th, 2026
Is DPU AIDAT exam conducted as an online or offline exam?
DPU AIDAT 2026 is conducted in online proctored mode — candidates appear for the test at their home/location through a laptop or desktop with a webcam enabled.
What is the eligibility criteria for DPU AIDAT?
B.Des Program- Candidates must be 10 + 2 from any discipline and from a recognized university or board with a minimum of 45% for Open and minimum of 40% for others. Freshers may also apply.
B.Sc. Hons. Program - Candidates must be 10 + 2 from any discipline and from a recognized university or board with a minimum of 45% for Open and minimum of 40% for others. Freshers may also apply.
What is the pattern of DPU AIDAT exam?
DPU AIDAT comprises 100 questions for 100 marks to be completed in 90 minutes. It includes questions based on Visualization, Observation & Perception, Environmental Awareness, Mental Ability, Creativity, Design Sustainability, Communication Skills and Design Problem Solving
When will admit card be issued for the DPU AIDAT 2026 exam?
The DPU AIDAT admit card 2026 will be issued one week before the exam date. Candidates need to download it from the official website dpu.edu.in using their login credentials and are advised to take a coloured printout to carry on the day of examination.

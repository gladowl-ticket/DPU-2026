DPGU School of Management & Research

MBA

    The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a rigorous two-year postgraduate program designed to develop advanced managerial competencies and strategic decision-making skills. Structured across four semesters, the program provides an in-depth understanding of core management disciplines while allowing students to specialize in key areas of business. The curriculum blends theoretical foundations with practical application through case studies, simulations, hands-on projects, and industry exposure, preparing students to excel in complex and competitive business environments.
    Students gain holistic knowledge of management principles, leadership practices, business analytics, financial decision-making, operations excellence, organizational behavior, and strategic planning. The program is specifically designed to foster critical thinking, innovation, and the ability to solve real-world business challenges. By the end of the program, graduates are equipped with the capability and confidence to lead teams, manage business functions, and contribute effectively to organizational growth across diverse sectors.

  • Graduate from any recognised University/ Institution of National Importance with a minimum of 45% marks or equivalent grade (40% marks or equivalent grade for Scheduled Caste /Scheduled Tribes) and appeared in at least one of the relevant entrance examinations like MH CET, CAT, CMAT, XAT, MAT, ATMA, PERA CET, Entrance Test of DPGU (DPAT).
